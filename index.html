Violent Bot

This is a discord bot for the MMORPG Tibia.

Use it to track allied & enemy guilds and monitor all deaths and levels on whatever worlds you choose.

Add To Discord

Online List

The online list has separate channels for:

  • Allies
  • Enemies
  • Neutrals

The channel name shows an online counter and updates every 5~ minutes.

Levels Feed

The levels feed is a combined channel that shows advancements by everyone.
It is more powerful than Nabbot's levels feed as you can see every level made on the server and can track enemies who are actively hunting more precisely.

Deaths Feed

The deaths feed will show all deaths on the server. This is probably the most valuable feature the bot provides. Deaths are marked depending on if its an enemy, ally or neutral and if its a PVE death or a PVP kill.

Activity Feed

The activity channel will show all guild joins/leaves & player name changes.

Automatic Enemy Detection

The bot activity channel will show all commands run using the bot.
It will also show when the bot has automatically detected an enemy and has added them to the hunted list.

Important Deaths

You can opt-in to important events, like if anyone dies to a rare spawning nemesis boss, or if an enemy dies fullbless. Using these tools ensures your team is in control of what matters on the worlds you play.

Privacy Policy

References
When referring to "personal data", your discord id and other information accessible to every other discord user is meant as well as personal information you pass on via command usages.

Collection of data
As config, your discords guild id and the channel ids of the channels the bot creates - are stored to a database.

Distribution of data
None of your data is being distributed to anybody else.

Deletion of data
All user-related data is deleted when the bot is kicked from your Discord server.
